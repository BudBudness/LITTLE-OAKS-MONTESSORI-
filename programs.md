# Our Programs

Little Oaks Montessori offers three progressive learning programs designed for children aged 1.5 to 6 years. Each program is tailored to meet the developmental needs of different age groups.

## Baby Class

**Ages: 1.5 – 2.5 years**

Our Baby Class provides a gentle introduction to the Montessori environment. Children develop:

- Sensory exploration skills
- Basic motor skills
- Language foundations
- Social awareness

## Middle Class

**Ages: 2.5 – 4 years**

The Middle Class builds on foundational skills with more structured activities:

- Practical life exercises
- Sensorial materials
- Language development
- Early mathematics concepts
- Art and creative expression

## Top Class

**Ages: 4 – 6 years**

Our Top Class prepares children for primary school with advanced learning:

- Reading and writing readiness
- Mathematics and number concepts
- Science and nature exploration
- Cultural studies
- Social and emotional preparation for school
