# About Little Oaks Montessori

Little Oaks Montessori is a child-centered early learning center in Nyamitanga, Mbarara, Uganda. Founded and directed by **Letica M. Byamugisha**, our school provides a nurturing Montessori environment for children aged 1.5 to 6 years.

## Our Philosophy

We believe that every child is unique and capable of learning at their own pace. Our Montessori approach focuses on:

- **Independence** – Encouraging children to do things for themselves
- **Creativity** – Fostering imagination and artistic expression
- **Emotional Growth** – Building confidence and emotional intelligence
- **Practical Life Skills** – Teaching real-world skills through hands-on activities

## Our Mission

To nurture confident, independent and emotionally balanced children.

## Our Vision

To provide a world-class Montessori foundation in Uganda.

## What Makes Us Special

- Small class sizes for personalized attention
- Trained Montessori educators
- Safe and stimulating learning environment
- Focus on the whole child – cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development

## School Hours

- **Weekdays:** 7:30 AM – 5:00 PM
- **Weekends:** Closed

## Location

Nyamitanga, Mbarara, Uganda
