# Admissions

We welcome new families to Little Oaks Montessori throughout the year. Our admissions process is designed to help us understand your child's needs and ensure a good fit with our program.

## Enrollment Requirements

- Children must be between 1.5 and 6 years of age
- Completed application form
- Birth certificate copy
- Immunization records
- Recent passport-size photographs

## Admissions Process

### Step 1: Inquiry
Contact us to learn more about our programs and schedule a school tour.

### Step 2: School Tour
Visit our campus to see our classrooms, meet our teachers, and experience our Montessori environment.

### Step 3: Application
Submit the completed application form along with required documents.

### Step 4: Assessment
We conduct a brief assessment to understand your child's developmental needs.

### Step 5: Enrollment
Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment by paying the registration fee and first term fees.

## What to Bring on the First Day

- Change of clothes
- Water bottle
- Healthy snacks (if not using school meals)
- Any comfort items for younger children

## School Calendar

Our academic year runs in three terms:
- **Term 1:** February – May
- **Term 2:** June – August
- **Term 3:** September – December
