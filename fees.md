# School Fees

Little Oaks Montessori offers competitive fees that reflect the quality of our Montessori education and care. All fees are payable per term unless otherwise stated.

## Fee Structure

### Registration Fee (One-time)
Payable upon enrollment to secure your child's place.

### Tuition Fees (Per Term)

| Class | Half Day | Full Day |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Baby Class | Contact for rates | Contact for rates |
| Middle Class | Contact for rates | Contact for rates |
| Top Class | Contact for rates | Contact for rates |

### What's Included

- Montessori materials and learning resources
- Qualified and trained teachers
- Daily meals and snacks (Full Day)
- Art and craft supplies
- Indoor and outdoor activities
- Progress reports

### Optional Services

- Transport (additional fee based on location)
- Extended hours care
- Holiday programs

## Payment Terms

- Fees are due at the beginning of each term
- Payment plans available upon request
- All payments should be made before the start of term

## Payment Methods

- Cash
- Bank transfer
- Mobile money
