# Welcome to Little Oaks Montessori

**A Montessori-based early childhood center focused on independence, creativity and emotional growth.**

Little Oaks Montessori Daycare & Nursery is located in Nyamitanga, Mbarara, Uganda. We provide a safe, joyful and Montessori-based learning environment for children aged 1.5–6 years.

## Our Mission

To nurture confident, independent and emotionally balanced children.

## Our Vision

To provide a world-class Montessori foundation in Uganda.

## Programs We Offer

- **Baby Class** – For our youngest learners
- **Middle Class** – Building foundational skills
- **Top Class** – Preparing for primary school
