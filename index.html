About Us

Welcome to Little Oaks Montessori! We are dedicated to providing a nurturing, stimulating and safe environment for children to learn and thrive. Our school embraces the Montessori philosophy, encouraging independence, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Programs

  • Daycare: Full-day care for young children, age 6 months and above.
  • Nursery School: Montessori-inspired pre-primary education for ages 2 to 6.
  • Enrichment Activities: Arts, crafts, music, and outdoor play to support whole-child development.
